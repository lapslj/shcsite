ABOUT US

I’m John. I started Summerhall because outsourced finance support is often the best way for small- to mid-sized charities to do the most good. Summerhall aims to be the nonprofit finance support that just works.

If you’re interested in our services, or even if you just want to chat - please reach out! Some of the most interesting and inspiring folks work at nonprofits, and I love meeting them. Email us at hello@summerhallconsulting.com.

Outsourced finance is often the best model for small- to mid-size nonprofits: fewer dollars on overhead means more capacity for your mission. But over 10+ years in nonprofit finance, I was mystified to see charities run in the the same problems again and again:

  • lack of knowledge transfer, between consultants or from consultants to staff
  • opacity on processes, hours, and billing
  • "one-size-fits-all" when you need specialized attention; unexpected add-ons when you need scale & savings
These factors can lead to costly over-dependence on consultants and errors that threaten your tax-exempt status. Outsourced support should free you up to focus on your programs, not force you to constantly retrain your consultants and check their work.

We pride ourselves on transparency, resource-consciousness, and a teaching-based approach. We take time to learn your business's unique situation; we develop clear workplans and solutions that match your needs with your resources; and we document our work & processes extensively so that whoever comes after us isn't grasping in the dark. Whatever your role, and whatever the length of our time with you, we want to leave you more capable and confident in your finances, not dependent on billable hours for every single question.
